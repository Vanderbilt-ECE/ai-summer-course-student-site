# Machine Learning Fundamentals

## How Models Learn - Loss and Gradient Descent

How a model finds good parameters: loss functions, gradient descent, and the learning rate, explained with no calculus required.

## Training Testing and Overfitting

Why testing a model on its own training data always looks good and tells you nothing — and how train/test splits catch overfitting.

## What ML Cannot Do - Limits and Blindspots

Where ML breaks: distribution shift, spurious correlations, and other limits of learning purely from data.

## What is Machine Learning

The paradigm shift from traditional rule-based programming to machines that improve at a task from data, without being explicitly programmed.
