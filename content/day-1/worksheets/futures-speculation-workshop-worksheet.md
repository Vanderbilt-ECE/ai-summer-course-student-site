# Futures Speculation Workshop Worksheet

**Pick your group:** healthcare, law & policy, art & design, business & finance, education, engineering, social sciences, or other.

## Question 1

## Question 2

What new roles or skills will emerge in this field because of AI?

## Question 3

What is one thing a human in this field will always be better at than AI?
